We are pleased to announce The Dynamic Radio Sky 2025 to be held in the week 28 July - 1 August in the vibrant city of Sydney, Australia. With recent advances and discoveries in radio astronomy, this meeting will bring together astronomers from around the world to share their research and insights across various aspects of the field. For those unable to attend in person, presentations and talks will be recorded and uploaded to the conference Slack workspace.

Date: 28 July - 1 August, 2025

Location: University of Sydney

The conference will primarily feature discussions of eight key topics in time domain radio astronomy:

  • Extragalactic synchrotron transients
  • Stellar radio emission
  • Galactic transients
  • Fast Radio Bursts
  • Active Galactic Nuclei and scintillation
  • Current and future surveys
  • Software and methods
  • Multi-wavelength synergies
